LASAGNA

Description

Lasagne are a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets.

Ingredients:
  • tomato
  • cheese
  • pomodori
Preparation process:
  1. Spread a thin layer of pasta sauce in the bottom of a baking dish.
  2. Make a layer of cooked lasagna noodles.
  3. Spread an even layer of the ricotta cheese mixture.
  4. Top it with a final layer of noodles, sauce, mozzarella, and parmesan cheese.
